Livestock Bank

Currently separate entity, yet owned and operated by CEO Herve Tuyishime. Livestock Bank takes a unique approach to this problem: We provide the best breed of pigs, goats, sheep and rabbits to farmers, who raise them with our support. Then we buy back the meat they produce. We ensure that animals’ production expenses are covered; including the cost of feed, water, medical/veterinary services, and everything else farmers need to grow healthy animals.

Why Livestock Bank?

The goal of the livestock bank is to create a research center laboratory that will help farmers and the country in large to select real gen and productive type of breed mainly with the aim to reduce costs of importing exotic breeds and facilitate the farmers to select their favorite breeds easily. In addition to this the laboratory will be built in large area where the offsprings will grow and technicians make comparison among them during their maturity age .however, this laboratory will be more important locally and nationally.

This laboratory will be located in Rukumberi sector – Ngoma district in eastern province, this is where we shall be testing the traits and genes and the area has medium temperature which is very essential in storage of semen.

Other products and services

The other goal of the livestock bank is to reach farmers, training and teaching them to grow livestock as a career, while earning them some extra income.

Connecting farmers, facilitating their access to products like biogas systems for their homes and giving them microloans for education and construction.

Helping farmers produce the best quality of animals in quantity then we become their market where farmers sell their product

Controlling the value chain farmers sell their production on time and We ensure the production reach to market with the quality market needs
