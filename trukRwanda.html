Why Truk Rwanda?

One of the challenges that the meat sector in Rwanda facing is the lack of enough transport vehicles to carry meat to the market either within the country or to the neighboring countries. As Paniel group has got refrigerated trucks we are going to use that opportunity to carry meat to different parties of the country to the supplier to our clients and also we will transport meat for the local businesses to deliver to their customers.
